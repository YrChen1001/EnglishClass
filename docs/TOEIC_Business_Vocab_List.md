## 7. 其他高頻片語 (High-Frequency Phrases)

*   **in the long run**: 從長遠來看 (ultimately, over a long period)
*   **rest assured**: 請放心 (be certain, have confidence)
*   **account for**: 考慮到；說明；佔(比例) (consider; explain)
*   **attend to**: 處理；照料 (deal with, take care of)
*   **coming soon**: 即將推出 (available shortly)
*   **make it**: 趕上；成功；到達 (succeed; arrive)
